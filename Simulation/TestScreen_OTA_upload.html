How to create the .bin from Arduino IDE

StepAction
1Select the correct ESP32 board and port.
2Select an OTA-capable partition scheme, for example “Default 4MB with OTA”.
3Flash the OTA-capable firmware once over USB.
4Use Sketch → Export Compiled Binary.
5Use Sketch → Show Sketch Folder and pick the generated app .bin, typically YourSketch.ino.bin.
6Do not use bootloader, partitions, or merged factory .bin for this OTA upload.
